# **App Name**: Zuliadog

## Core Features:

- Promotional Hero Section: A compelling title, concise subtitle, main call-to-action button to Google Play, and an image/mockup showcasing the app.
- Key Features Overview: Three distinct cards highlighting core app functionalities: medical record tracking, vaccination control, and pet profiles.
- Data Privacy & Deletion Information: A dedicated section explaining user data control, transparent deletion instructions for Google Play compliance, and a mailto link.
- App Download Call-to-Action: A prominent and clickable 'Descargar en Google Play' button, ready for a direct store link.
- Legal & Copyright Footer: A footer including the copyright for a 'Compañía Anónima' and placeholder links for Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A friendly and professional blue (#3581A8), inspired by the calm and trust of medical blues and clear skies. (HSL: 195, 60%, 45%)
- Background color: A subtle, very light cool grey (#F4F7F8), derived from the primary hue to maintain cleanliness and openness. (HSL: 195, 15%, 96%)
- Accent color: A fresh and vibrant aquamarine (#3EC9AA), analogous to the primary and adding a touch of liveliness that evokes well-being. (HSL: 165, 70%, 55%)
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' (humanist sans-serif) for its modern, clean, and warm readability, suitable for both headlines and body text.
- Utilize simple, clean line-art icons that playfully yet professionally represent pet care and app features (e.g., a paw print, a medical cross, a calendar).
- A responsive single-page layout with clear sectioning. A hero banner, followed by a three-column feature display for key information, and distinct sections for privacy and footer content. Mobile-first design principles will ensure optimal viewing on smaller screens.
- Subtle, fluid animations on hover effects for interactive elements like buttons and card components to enhance user engagement. Gentle fade-in transitions for sections as they become visible on scroll for a smooth browsing experience.
